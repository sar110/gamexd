Top tips for small gaming youtubers


Gaming is a competitive YouTube niche, but there's still room (and time) for new creators to grow successful channels.


Tip 1: Take the time to come up with a unique idea and make the right choices when you start.

Tip 2: Think about how you will structure your videos and the length of the videos.

Tip 3: Work on the intro of your video carefully.
Tip 4: Thumbnails should be simple and colourful.
Tip 5: Keep your idea of channel growth simple.
